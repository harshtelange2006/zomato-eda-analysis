# Zomato Bengaluru Restaurant Analysis

## Problem

Bengaluru is a Tier 1 city with a massive food delivery market with 51,000+ restaurant listings on Zomato.

---

## Approach

- Dataset: 51,000 Zomato restaurant listings from Bengaluru
- Tool: Python (Pandas, Matplotlib/Seaborn)
- Method: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) — cleaning the data, finding distributions, comparing groups, and extracting patterns

---

## Findings

### 1. Market is built on convenience

Quick Bites and Casual Dining together make up **73% of all restaurant types**. Bengaluru's food market is not fine dining — it's fast, affordable, and convenient.

### 2. North Indian cuisine dominates delivery

The majority of orders are placed via delivery, and **North Indian cuisine appears in 32.7% of listings** — the single most common cuisine category on the platform.

### 3. Average rating sits at 3.7

Most restaurants cluster around a **3.7 rating**, and these are predominantly dine-in establishments. This sets a clear benchmark — anything above 4.0 stands out.

### 4. BTM Layout is the most competitive area

**BTM Layout holds 13.7% of all restaurant listings** — the highest concentration of any single area in Bengaluru. High supply means high competition for restaurants operating there.

### 5. Table booking correlates with higher ratings

Restaurants that accept table bookings have **above-average ratings**. The likely reason: pre-booked dining creates a more intentional experience, which customers rate more favorably.

### 6. Byg Brewski Brewing Company is the most voted restaurant

Among all 51,000 listings, **Byg Brewski** (a brewing company) received the highest number of votes — signaling strong brand loyalty and word-of-mouth.

### 7. Sankey Road is a premium dining pocket

**Average cost for two in Sankey Road is higher than most other areas**, suggesting it attracts a more affluent customer base or hosts premium restaurant formats.

---

## Business Impact

- A new restaurant entering Bengaluru should **avoid BTM Layout** unless differentiated — it's oversaturated.
- **North Indian cuisine + delivery** is the safest combo for volume, but also the most crowded.
- Adding **table booking** is a low-effort feature that may improve perceived quality and ratings.
- Targeting **Sankey Road** makes sense for premium concepts with higher price points.
- The **3.7 benchmark** means getting to 4.0+ is a real competitive advantage on the platform.
